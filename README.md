# Local Mosque Prayer Times Support

Welcome to the official support page for **Local Mosque Prayer Times**!

Local Mosque Prayer Times is your all-in-one Islamic lifestyle companion designed to help you stay connected to your faith throughout the day. It provides accurate salah timings, a reliable Qibla direction compass, a smart mosque locator, and a dhikr counter.

If you have any questions, encounter issues, or want to request a new feature, you are in the right place.

**Q: How are prayer times calculated?**  
A: The app automatically calculates prayer times based on your precise location using trusted Islamic calculation methods (such as Umm al-Qura, ISNA, MWL, Egyptian General Authority). You can adjust the calculation method in the settings to match your preference.

**Q: How do I find the Qibla direction?**  
A: Simply open the Qibla tab to access the built-in compass. It provides a precise direction towards Mecca, working both indoors and outdoors.

**Q: Can I find nearby mosques?**  
A: Yes! The Mosque Finder feature helps you discover prayer halls, Islamic centers, and masjids around you with detailed maps and directions.

**Q: How do I use the Dhikr Counter?**  
A: You can use the built-in Dhikr Counter to perform tasbeeh and zikr. It allows you to track repetitions, set goals, and maintain your daily remembrance practices.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**  
A: The Dhikr Counter works completely offline. However, features like Prayer Times, Qibla Direction, and Mosque Finder require an internet connection to provide accurate data based on your location.
